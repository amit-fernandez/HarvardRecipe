Chicken Biriyani




Chicken Marination
Chicken2 lbs
Chilli Powder4 tspn
Salt2 tspn
Corriandar Powder3 tspn
Turmeric Powder1/4 tspn
Garam Masala1/2 tspn
Pepper Powder1/4 tspn
Ginger Garlic Paste3 tspn
Lemon1
Curd1 cup
Corriandar leaves1 Bunch
Mint leaves1 Bunch
Fried Onions1/2 cup
Oil4 oz



For Biriyani
Basmathi Rice6 cups
Whole Spices1/2 tspn
Salt 2 tpsn
Bay Leaf
Ghee 2 tspn
Oil 4 oz
Water 7 cups




1. Marinate the items listed in marination with 2lbs of chicken for 4 hours or overnight
2. Add all the items in "For Biriyani" list and cook until rice is half done
3. Preheat oven to 450 C
4. Take a aluminium tray and apply oil on that
5. Evenly spread the marinated chicken and spread the cooked rice on top of that
6. Cook for 1 hour
