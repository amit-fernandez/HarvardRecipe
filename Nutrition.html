Benefits

Almonds

  • Highest in calcium of all nuts
  • Almonds are also high in fiber, vitamin E and magnesium
  • Almonds help lower cholesterol and reduce the risk of heart disease
  • help protect against diabetes

Hazelnuts

  • Rich in unsaturated fats (mostly oleic acid), high in magnesium, calcium and vitamins B and E

  • Hazelnuts are good for your heart,

  • help reduce the risk of cancer, and aid in muscle, skin, bone, joint and digestive health.

Cashews

  • Rich in iron, high in magnesium (more than almonds),
  • Cashews help prevent cancer, promote a healthy heart and strong bones
  • also are good for your skin and hair (they are rich in copper).
